Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 20, 2026

One-line summary

FlowMen does not collect, store, or share any information about you, period. Everything stays on your device. No exceptions.

Who we are

FlowMen is an app developed by Fabiano Gadenz (NewPrompt) and distributed for free on the App Store and Google Play. This document explains how the app handles data — and, more importantly, what it does not do.

What we collect

Nothing. No data collection whatsoever.

This includes:

  • No name, email, phone number, or any contact info.
  • No location.
  • No device identifiers (IDFA, Advertising ID, device ID).
  • No usage data (analytics, telemetry, crash reports, heat maps).
  • Nothing you type into the app (daily entries, reasons, notes, dates, partner names, cycles).

Where your data lives

Exclusively on your device's internal storage.

Everything you enter in the app — daily records, partner names, special dates, cycles, preferences — is saved locally using a store called GetStorage, inside the app's private folder on your device.

That folder is accessible only to FlowMen. Other apps, the operating system, and we (the developers) have no access to it.

If you uninstall the app, the data is gone with it.

What FlowMen does NOT do

  • Does not send any data to servers (ours or third-party).
  • Does not have login or registration.
  • Does not use Google Analytics, Firebase, Amplitude, Mixpanel, Sentry, or any other analytics / crash reporting tool.
  • Does not show ads.
  • Does not sell or share data with partners.
  • Does not connect to social networks.
  • Does not back up automatically to any cloud.

Internet connection

FlowMen works fully offline. The only exception is:

  • Google Fonts — the first time the app runs, it may download the Manrope font file over HTTPS from Google servers. After that, the font is cached locally and the download does not repeat. This download carries no information about you; it's just a request for a public file. Google, acting as CDN, may log the request's IP address according to their own privacy policy (policies.google.com/privacy).

If you prefer to avoid this download, you can run the app offline — the system falls back to the device's default font.

Backup export

The app has an "Export data" function (Settings → Export data) that generates a text blob with all your data. The blob is placed on your clipboard and you decide where to send it (another app, email, file). "Import data" does the opposite.

This feature is for you to move data between devices or keep a manual backup. We, the developers, have no access to that backup at any point.

Cookies, trackers, and similar technologies

None used. FlowMen is a native iOS/Android app with no web components, no cookies, and no trackers.

Children's privacy

FlowMen is not directed at children under 12. We do not knowingly collect data from children — and, considering the app collects no data from anyone, that includes children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes in the future, the new version will be posted here with an updated date at the top. If a change materially affects how the app handles data, a note will be included in the app's next update (under "What's New" in the App Store / Play Store).
